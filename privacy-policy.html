Important Note on Content Sources

The GramPlayer app does not facilitate downloading, converting, or streaming content from unauthorised third-party sources such as YouTube, Instagram, or similar services. Instead, it is designed to work exclusively with the users personal Telegram account using Telegram official TDLib API.

  • The app allows only the authenticated Telegram user to log in with their own account.
  • Files and media accessible in the app come solely from the user Telegram chats, channels, or groups they already belong to.
  • No external or unauthorised third-party content sources are used.
  • The app follows Telegram official API/TDLib usage guidelines.

In other words, the app functions as a personal Telegram media manager for the signed-in user and does not provide or promote unauthorised downloads from any third-party platforms.
